# Indicator Overview

The Macro Forecast Hub tracks 12 monthly U.S. macroeconomic indicators from
the FRED-MD dataset. Four are required for all submissions; eight are optional.

---

## Required Indicators

These must be included in every forecast submission.

### INDPRO — Industrial Production Index

- **Category:** Real Activity
- **Units:** Index, 2017=100
- **Seasonal Adjustment:** Yes
- **Description:** Measures real output for manufacturing, mining, electric,
  and gas utilities. A key coincident indicator of the business cycle.

### UNRATE — Civilian Unemployment Rate

- **Category:** Labor Market
- **Units:** Percent
- **Seasonal Adjustment:** Yes
- **Description:** Percentage of the labor force that is unemployed. One of
  the most closely watched labor market indicators.

### PAYEMS — Total Nonfarm Payrolls

- **Category:** Labor Market
- **Units:** Thousands of persons
- **Seasonal Adjustment:** Yes
- **Description:** Total number of nonfarm employees. The headline employment
  figure from the monthly jobs report.

### CPIAUCSL — Consumer Price Index

- **Category:** Prices
- **Units:** Index, 1982-84=100
- **Seasonal Adjustment:** Yes
- **Description:** Measures the average change in prices paid by urban consumers
  for a basket of goods and services. The most widely cited inflation measure.

---

## Optional Indicators

### PCEPI — PCE Price Index

- **Category:** Prices
- **Units:** Index, 2017=100
- **Description:** The Federal Reserve's preferred inflation measure. Broader
  coverage than CPI and accounts for substitution effects.

### FEDFUNDS — Federal Funds Rate

- **Category:** Interest Rates
- **Units:** Percent
- **Description:** The interest rate at which banks lend reserves to each other
  overnight. The primary tool of Federal Reserve monetary policy.

### GS10 — 10-Year Treasury Rate

- **Category:** Interest Rates
- **Units:** Percent
- **Description:** Yield on 10-year U.S. Treasury bonds. A benchmark for
  mortgage rates and long-term borrowing costs.

### TB3MS — 3-Month Treasury Bill Rate

- **Category:** Interest Rates
- **Units:** Percent
- **Description:** Short-term risk-free rate. Closely tracks the federal funds
  rate and reflects near-term monetary policy expectations.

### HOUST — Housing Starts

- **Category:** Housing
- **Units:** Thousands of units
- **Description:** New privately-owned housing units started. A leading
  indicator of economic activity and construction sector health.

### M2SL — M2 Money Stock

- **Category:** Money & Credit
- **Units:** Billions of USD
- **Description:** Broad measure of the money supply including cash, checking
  deposits, savings deposits, and money market funds.

### DPCERA3M086SBEA — Real Personal Consumption

- **Category:** Real Activity
- **Units:** Billions of chained 2017 USD
- **Description:** Consumer spending adjusted for inflation. The largest
  component of GDP (~70%).

### RETAILx — Retail Sales

- **Category:** Real Activity
- **Units:** Millions of USD
- **Description:** Total retail and food services sales. A high-frequency
  indicator of consumer spending patterns.
